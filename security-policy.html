Our Security Policy

Your privacy and security are very important to us. Please review our security guidelines below:

1. Identification & Authentication

To protect your account, we use secure authentication methods. This ensures that only authorized users can access their accounts.

2. Password Policy

We require strong passwords to enhance security. Please create passwords that are at least 8 characters long and include numbers, uppercase letters, and symbols.

3. Acceptable Use Policy (AUP)

By using Baketopia services, users agree to follow our Acceptable Use Policy, maintaining a safe and secure environment for everyone.

4. Remote Access

Remote access to Baketopia systems is granted only to authorized personnel using secure methods such as VPNs and multi-factor authentication.

5. Network Maintenance

We regularly maintain our network to apply the latest security updates and ensure the reliability of our services.

6. Incident Handling Procedure

If a security incident occurs, we follow a detailed incident response plan to quickly address and mitigate risks. Users will be notified as needed.
